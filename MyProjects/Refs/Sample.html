Welcome Copa Surf Tournament!

Copa Surf Registration:

The tournament will take place over Presidents Day Weekend, Feb ruary 14-17, 2025. The Copa Surf tournament is an Elite showcase and Qualifier tournament for competitive clubs across the Northwest region, including western Canada. Copa Surf offers an automatic spot in Surf Cup San Diego for winners of the Platinum division, application fees not covered.

AGE GROUPS:

U8 U19 Boys and Girls

FORMAT:

Minimum of 3 games

U8-U10 7v7, 25min Halves

U11-U12 9v9, 30min Halves

U13-U19 11v11, 35min Halves

DATES:

February 14-17, 2025

REGISTRATION DEADLINE:
January 17, 2025

LOCATION:

STARFIRE SPORTS COMPLEX
